Privacy Policy

This policy explains how SCAI collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection

SCAI does not collect personal information. The tool stores assessment answers locally on your device and does not transmit them to any server.

2. Use of Data

The data entered is used solely to calculate the assessment score and generate the PDF report.

3. Data Security

Data is stored locally in the browser (localStorage). Users should avoid using public/shared devices.

4. Third Party

This tool does not share data with third parties. No tracking or analytics are used.

5. User Rights

Users may delete the data by clearing browser storage or using the Reset option in the assessment page.
